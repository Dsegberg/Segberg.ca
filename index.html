How it works

No extra platforms. Uses simple, school-friendly tools.

RFID / NFC Students tap a card or tag for quick check-in.
Google Sheets School-owned data backend for easy updates and exports.
Google Apps Script Web app delivery with controlled access and PIN protection.

Smart Seat opens as a web app, while your data stays organized behind the scenes in Google Sheets.
