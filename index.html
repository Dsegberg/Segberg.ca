Segberg.ca Educator-built classroom tools

Smart, visible classroom management.

Smart Seat is a web-based classroom tool that handles attendance, seat assignment, and student in/out tracking in real time — designed for clarity, speed, and daily classroom use.

Built by an educator using tools schools already trust.

Launch Smart Seat Who it’s for
RFID Attendance Fast tap-in attendance with automatic timestamps.
Seat Assignment Assign or randomize seating instantly for classes or exams.
In / Out Tracking Live visibility of students leaving and returning.
Built for Visibility Designed to be read from anywhere in the room.

Who it’s for

Teachers Daily attendance and seating without interruptions.
Exam Supervision Clear layouts and quick seat resets.
Substitute Teachers Simple launch-and-use workflow.

How it works

RFID / NFC Students tap cards or tags to check in.
Google Sheets School-owned data backend.
Google Apps Script Secure, PIN-protected web app.
