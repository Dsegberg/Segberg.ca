Smart Seat RFID Attendance & Assign

Smart, visible classroom management.

Smart Seat is a classroom web app that handles attendance, seat assignment, and student in/out tracking in real time — without slowing down instruction.

Launch Smart Seat Who it’s for
RFID Attendance Fast tap-in attendance with automatic timestamps.
Seat Assignment Assign or randomize seating instantly for daily classes or exams.
In / Out Tracking Clear, live visibility of students leaving and returning.
Built for Visibility Designed to be read from anywhere in the room.

Who it’s for

TeachersDaily attendance and seating without interruptions.
Exam supervisionClear layouts and quick seat resets.
Substitute teachersSimple launch-and-use workflow.

How it works

RFID / NFCStudents tap cards or tags.
Google SheetsSchool-owned data backend.
Google Apps ScriptSecure, PIN-protected web app.
