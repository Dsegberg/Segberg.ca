What Smart Seat does

Smart Seat is designed to reduce classroom friction—fewer interruptions, fewer manual steps, and more clarity for both students and staff.

Tap-in Attendance

Quick scanning and automatic timestamps. Great for busy classes and early-bird programs.

Seating

Smart Seat Assign

In / Out Visibility
